League of Legends Game modes:

There are three pvp(player vs player) modes and two co op(player vs computer) modes.I did not show the twisted treeline map because no one really plays that game mode and you probably won't either.The goal in these game modes is to kill the enemy nexus (the large square thing at the end on the base). You have towers to protect your champion and minions to help you along the way. After you kill a player or minion you get game gold which you can spend on items to get stronger.
