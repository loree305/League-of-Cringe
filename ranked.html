What is ranked and should I do it?

Ranked is a more intense game mode. It's exactly like the normal game modes but you get judged on your game play and your wins/losses. Should you do it? Absolutely! It helps you become a better player and gives you an idea of what role you should play.

How do I rise in ranks?

You play ranked games and you gain or lose a currency called LP. When you collect 100 LP you go up to placements. If you win 2/3 games you go on to the next rank and it continues. The picture below shows how to make a ranked game.
